About Us

Introduction: Justice & Civil Society Support Organization (JCSSO) is a national non-governmental, non-profit, and non-political organization. JCSSO’s mandate is to seek a world of hope, tolerance and social justice; where poverty has been overcome and people live in dignity, justice and security. JCSSO was established/registered with Afghan Ministry of Economy in 2009 as NNGO. JCSSO puts people's rights, including humanitarian principles, values, transparency and accountability, at the center of all performances because we know that we cannot overcome poverty and social injustice until all people have equal rights and opportunities. JCSSO is aimed to tackle humanitarian crises, including droughts and disaster prevention, and develops basic community social services for youth, women, IDPs, and poor rural communities to reduce poverty and contribute to National Development Goals.
