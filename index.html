About Us

Introduction: Introduction: Justice & Civil Society Support Organization (JCSSO) is a national non-governmental, non-profit, and non-political organization JCSSO’s mandate is to seek a world of hope, tolerance and social justice; where poverty has been overcome and people live in dignity, justice and security. JCSSO stablished/registered with Afghan ministry of economy in 2009 as NNGO. JCSSO puts people rights including humanitarian principle, values, transparency and accountability in the center of all performances because we know that we cannot overcome poverty and social injustice until all people have no equal rights and opportunities JCSSO is aimed to tackle the humanitarian crisis including droughts and disaster prevention and develops community basic social Services needs for the Youth, Women, IDPs and poor rural community to reduce the level of poverty and contribute National Development Goals.
